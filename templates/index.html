Learn to Play

GAME PLAY

Players take turns moving their respective pieces. During a player's turn, they must

  • place a token on their piece’s current square, AND
  • make a queen’s move to any square on the board that is not blocked by a chip, the opponent’s piece, or the arrow, AND
  • move the arrow to lie on the board, connecting the previous square with the current. If they moves their piece directly towards the opponent, they does not place the arrow, but instead, removes it from the board (this move is called an "open").

OBJECTIVE

Trap the opponent: the first player unable to make a move, loses, and the opponent wins.
